Emirati Sign Language Generative AI Global Summit

About

Emirati Sign Language (ESL) AI Global Summit is a global event, organized by Microsoft Tech Club, BITS Pilani Dubai during the International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Network Systems (CNIS) 2023 Dubai, held at BITS Pilani Dubai.
The summit aims to leverage the capabilities of generative AI to enhance accessibility and communication for the Deaf and hard-of-hearing community in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) through Emirati Sign Language (ESL).
